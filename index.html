Ritchy's Daily Life

I am a cute, gentle puppy that was found in a park in Shanghai and adopted by Ben and Natalie.

Every morning I make sure I'm on my mom and dad's bed - they need to know that I'm ready to take them on the first walk of the day!

I have lots of fun with my mom and dad outside! I don't fetch but I'm still very playful and silly.

I love meeting other dogs too! I have a few friends in our neighbourhood.
